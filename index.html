Why we started

Good ideas need people who will help move them forward.

Soni Family Foundation was created to support local organizations that already understand their communities and are ready to do more.

How we work

We do not want to fund from a distance.

We want to understand the need, stay involved, and learn with our partners.

  • Listen to the people closest to the work
  • Set a clear and realistic goal
  • Support delivery, not just the idea
  • Review what worked and what can improve
