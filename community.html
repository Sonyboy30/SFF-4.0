Partnerships

We want to build with people, not around them.

Our best partnerships start with an honest conversation about the need, the idea, and what meaningful progress could look like.

Who we work with

Local organizations serving young people

We are especially interested in schools, nonprofits, and community groups working in South and West Dallas.

A partnership can include funding, volunteer support, program planning, mentorship, or another form of collaboration that makes sense for the organization.

Share a partnership idea
Our process

What a first conversation looks like

Tell us the need

What are students, families, or staff experiencing right now?

Explain the idea

What would you like to try, improve, or expand?

Set a goal

What would a useful result look like in the near term?

Plan together

We decide whether there is a good fit and what support could help.

A good fit

We may be a good match if...

  • Your work directly supports young people or families
  • Your organization is connected to the community it serves
  • You have a clear problem or opportunity in mind
  • You are open to sharing feedback and outcomes
Before you reach out

Helpful information to include

  • A short description of your organization
  • The group of students or families you serve
  • The idea or need you want to discuss
  • Your preferred timeline
